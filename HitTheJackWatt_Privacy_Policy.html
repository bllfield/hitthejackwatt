Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

1. Information IntelliWatt Collects

IntelliWatt collects the following types of information from users:

  • Personal Information: such as name, email address, phone number, and zip code when you sign up or complete forms.
  • Energy Usage Data: when you authorize us to access your Smart Meter Texas account or provide usage details manually.
  • Referral and Entry Activity: including number of referrals, entries earned, and portal participation.

2. How IntelliWatt Uses Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Calculate and manage entries in the HitTheJackWatt monthly drawings.
  • Notify you of drawing results and new ways to earn entries.
  • Inform you of cost-saving energy opportunities, if available.

3. Sharing of Information

IntelliWatt does not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may only be shared with service providers as necessary to operate our platform (e.g., secure payment processors).

4. Data Retention

IntelliWatt retains your data only as long as needed to operate the program and maintain compliance with applicable regulations.

5. Your Choices

You may request to opt out of communications, delete your account, or update your information at any time by contacting us at privacy@intelli-watt.com.

6. Security

IntelliWatt uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Updates to This Policy

IntelliWatt may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@intelli-watt.com.
