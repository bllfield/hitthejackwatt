HitTheJackWatt Monthly Jackpot – Official Rules

1. No Purchase Necessary

No purchase, payment, or energy plan switch is required to enter or win. Void where prohibited. By participating, you agree to abide by these rules and all decisions made by Intelliwatt.

2. Eligibility

Open to legal U.S. residents age 18 or older. Must have a valid email address and mobile number. Employees of Intelliwatt or its affiliates are not eligible to win. Entry is subject to verification and may be void if fraudulent or incomplete.

3. How to Earn Entries

Each participant may earn entries through the following actions:

  • Sign up at HitTheJackWatt.com: 1 entry
  • Authorize Smart Meter Texas data pull: 10 entries
  • Complete home details form: 10 entries
  • Complete appliance details form: 10 entries
  • Refer a friend who signs up: 5 entries per signup

Max standard entries per user: 31
Unlimited bonus entries from valid referrals.
Once earned, entries remain valid for all future monthly drawings unless withdrawn by request.

4. Jackpot Amount

The monthly jackpot is $5 per successful energy plan switch completed through Intelliwatt during that month.

Example:

  • 200 switches = $1,000 jackpot
  • 1,200 switches = $6,000 jackpot

Note: You do not need to switch plans to enter or win - but the jackpot gets bigger every time someone does.

5. Drawing Periods

Drawings occur monthly, on or around the 5th of each month. Entry period runs from 12:00 AM CT on the 1st day through 11:59 PM CT on the last day of the month.

6. Winner Selection

One winner is chosen at random from all valid, cumulative entries.
Each entry is a separate chance to win.
Users stay entered month after month - 'One entry today is a chance every month, forever.'

"Even though more people are joining, your entry is permanent. It's like a free lottery ticket every month - forever."

7. Odds of Winning

Odds depend on the total number of eligible entries. They change monthly as more users and referrals join, but every valid entry remains active.
Example: 100,000 total entries = 1 in 100,000 odds per entry.

8. Winner Notification

Winner will be notified via email and/or SMS within 3 business days. Must respond within 7 days to claim the prize. Alternate winners may be selected if unclaimed.

9. Prize Delivery

Prizes are issued via digital payment (PayPal, Venmo, etc.) or mailed check, based on the winner's preference.

10. Additional Terms

By entering, you agree to receive occasional notifications from Intelliwatt - not sales pitches, just helpful updates.
We'll only reach out to share ways you may be able to save money, get more entries, or learn about new energy opportunities in your area.
No spam. No pressure. No strings attached.
